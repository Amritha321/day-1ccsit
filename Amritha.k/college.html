Welcome to CCSIT The Pareeksha Bhavan is the nerve centre of university examination. It conducts examinations for all courses of the University, arranges for the valuation of answer scripts, processes marks, and announces results and issues diplomas, degrees and certificates. The valuation of answer scripts of postgraduate semester courses and all degree courses is carried out at centralised valuation camps and results of all examinations are announced in time. To provide a better and efficient service to students, the examination related work is being computerised in a phased manner.
